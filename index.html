About Me

My name is Downey Huynh, and volleyball has been a major part of my life as both a player and a coach. I graduated from Fountain Valley High School in 2017, continued my playing career at Orange Coast College from 2017 to 2019, and later played at the University of Jamestown from 2020 to 2023.

Along the way, I built experience coaching across multiple levels of the game. I have worked with youth athletes, high school teams, club programs, and college players in both assistant and head coaching roles. My coaching background includes Fountain Valley High School, Edison High School, Balboa Bay Volleyball Club, and the University of Jamestown.

Through Seams Right Volleyball, I want to help athletes develop strong fundamentals, better confidence, and a deeper understanding of the game. My coaching style is clear, encouraging, and detail-oriented, with an emphasis on teaching skills that translate directly to real match play.
