About Me

I started volleyball late as a freshman in high school and didn’t have much guidance on how to navigate the sport—what clubs to join, how to train, or how to pursue playing at the next level. I didn’t play a single minute my freshman year, which forced me to work, train, and earn every opportunity that came after.

Through consistent effort and learning along the way, I was able to build a respectable playing career at Orange Coast College and later at the University of Jamestown. That journey—from not playing to competing at the collegiate level—shapes how I coach today.

My goal is to provide an affordable and clear path for athletes and parents who may feel confused about the volleyball journey. I focus on helping players build strong fundamentals, confidence, and real game skills while giving them the guidance I wish I had when I started.
